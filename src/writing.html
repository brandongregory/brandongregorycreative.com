Brandon writes articles.

I’m a writer of web and religious articles, as well as some fiction and technical documents. For most of my life, I’ve been working on one writing project or another, and it’s something I hope I keep doing until the day I die.

Professional articles have been published on A List Apart, and I self-publish some articles on Medium. I also review classic movies and write every other Tuesday for my church blog.

There is nothing to writing. All you do is sit down at a typewriter and bleed.
Brandon is always looking for new writing opportunities!
